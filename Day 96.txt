 Day 96. Grit. The spirit to keep punching. Grit is like being in a boxing ring with life. It's not always about how hard you can hit, but how many hits you can take and keep moving forward. It's about not letting a knockdown turn into a knockout. Every challenge you face is going to throw punches at you. Some will be soft, easy to dodge, while others might hit hard, knocking you down. But having grit means, even if you get knocked down, you never stay down. You keep punching, not because you're unafraid or unharmed, but because you're determined. You know that with each punch you throw back, you're one step closer to winning your fight, achieving your goals. So in the face of difficulties, remember, it's not about the strength of your punch, but the spirit that drives it. Keep punching, keep moving, and you'll see nothing can keep you down for long.
