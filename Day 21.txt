 Day 21. Gratitude. Pin gratitude to exude positivity. How about we bring back a bit of old school charm with thank you notes. Once a week, let's take a moment to pin down a few lines to someone who's made a difference in our week. It could be a friend, a teacher, or hey, even the barista who nails your coffee every time. It doesn't have to be fancy. Just a simple note or email to say, hey, I noticed what you did and I'm really thankful for it. It's a small act, but it can mean a lot. And you know what? It not only makes their day a bit brighter, but it feels pretty good for us too. It's like a little burst of positivity. And who knows, it might just cause others to show their gratitude too. Try it.
