 Day 84. Goals and dreams. Your life, your map. So, here's the thing. Life's full of expectations that other people have for us. It's like everyone's got a map they think we should follow. Maybe it's your parents, teachers, or even your friends. But the deal is, it's your life, your journey. Other people's maps are taken from their own experiences, their own fears, and what they think is best for you. But when it comes to living your life, you're the one putting one foot in front of the other. So, while it's smart to consider advice from people who care about you, remember, your life's your adventure, not theirs. It's okay to explore, make your own choices, and find your own way. Trust me, that's what makes life interesting and uniquely yours. And if you're a parent, don't insist that your kids follow your map.
