 Day 89. Self-care. Accept yourself. Warts and all. Self-acceptance is like giving yourself a big hug, recognizing that you're awesome just the way you are. It's about loving and embracing every part of you, even the warts, the bits you're not so happy about. Here's a simple idea to practice self-acceptance. Every day, stand in front of the mirror and say one thing you like about yourself. It could be anything. Maybe your sense of humour or how you helped a friend. For instance, if you did a good job in completing your homework or just made a killer omelette, acknowledge it. Say, hey, I did that and I did it really well. This is like giving yourself a pat on the back regularly. It boosts your confidence and helps you see yourself in a positive light. Remember, the more you appreciate yourself, the brighter your world becomes.
