 Day 76. Grit. A phrase to make you unshakable. Imagine having a secret phrase, like a magic spell, that boosts your strength whenever you hit a bump in the road. Sounds good? You can make one. It's your very own stay strong phrase. A mantra that's all about your unshakable spirit, crafted from words that get your heart pumping with courage. Then, whenever a challenge tries to knock you down, say your mantra out loud. Let those words be the echo in your mind that drowns out the noise of doubt. It's like your inner cheerleader reminding you that you've got what it takes to muscle through anything. And believe me, that little echo, it can move mountains within you. Keep at it, and you'll see. You're unstoppable.
