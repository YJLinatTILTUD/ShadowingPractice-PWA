 Day 22. Conquering adversity. Even the best athletes have coaches. You know that myth that says you should tackle everything alone? Well, that's just not true. Even the best athletes have coaches, right? They get that outside perspective to level up. Needing help isn't a sign you're lacking. It just means you're smart enough to get the right tools. So, if something's bothering you, whether it's your thoughts, skills, or finances, why not seek out an expert? Find a therapist, a coach, or a financial pro. These folks are equipped to help you overcome obstacles and thrive. Bringing in an outside perspective, sometimes that's the key. It's not about giving up. It's about teaming up to conquer whatever comes your way.
