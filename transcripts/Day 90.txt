 Day 90. Philosophy. Forgiveness is for you. There's this idea that time can heal everything. And, in a way, it's true. When someone does something that hurts us, we might feel really upset at first. But, as time passes, we sometimes find those hard feelings fading away. Yet, holding onto anger and resentment doesn't change what happened. It only makes it harder to forgive. Research shows these feelings can even mess with your health, like harming the immune system and triggering emotional gastrointestinal diseases, among other issues. So, what's the point in keeping them around? Here's a thought. Try forgiving everyone for everything, and do it now, not years later. It sounds tough, I know, but it's really for you, not them. Forgiving is like setting yourself free from carrying around all that heavy stuff. It's not about saying what they did was okay. It's about giving yourself the gift of moving on and finding happiness. When you let go of grudges, you make room for peace in your heart. And that's a pretty amazing feeling.
