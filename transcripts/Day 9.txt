 Day 9. Self-Care. Plant seeds of positivity for yourself. Starting and ending your day with positive thoughts is like nourishing your mind. It fills your day with confidence and good energy. It's a simple yet impactful way to remember your worth. Try this. When you wake up and before you sleep, think of good things about yourself. Maybe tell yourself, I like myself. I feel terrific. Doing it regularly helps shape a confident and upbeat mindset. The way you talk to yourself really shapes your day. Lift yourself up each morning and night. It makes a big difference in keeping the positivity flowing.
