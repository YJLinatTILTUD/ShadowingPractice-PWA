 Day 63. Learning. A watchpot never boils. Sometimes, progress is like a sneaky little ninja. You don't see it happening, but it's there. Even when you feel like you've hit a plateau, and your English isn't getting any better, trust me, it is. It's just that the results are hiding from you. Remember the old saying that a watchpot never boils? Whatever you do, don't constantly check to see if you're making progress. You could be spending that precious time practicing and learning. In fact, the less you care about instant improvement, and the more you simply enjoy the process, the faster you'll progress. Then, once you look back, you'll see just how far you've come. So if you're feeling stuck or like you're not improving, don't let that get to you. Just keep at it. Keep practicing and keep learning. The progress will show itself in time. And when it does, you'll be amazed at how much you've grown. So, chin up, believe in yourself, and charge on. You've got this.
