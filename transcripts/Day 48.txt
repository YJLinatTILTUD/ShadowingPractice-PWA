 Day 48. Growth. Add fuel to your growth fire. Imagine your personal growth as a campfire. The more would you add, the bigger and warmer it gets. Similarly, the pace at which you grow significantly depends on how often and how much effort you put into your personal growth fire. It's really straightforward. The more time and energy you dedicate, the quicker and fully your growth. It's about showing up every day, even if some days are harder than others. Let's say you want to get a six-pack. If you do crunches every day and gradually increase the number of reps you do each time, you'll have washboard abs in no time. Each effort, no matter how small, adds fuel to your fire. It's a continuous effort, sometimes exhausting, but it's so rewarding. So, keep piling on the efforts, keep fueling your fire, and you'll see how wonderfully warm and bright it can get.
