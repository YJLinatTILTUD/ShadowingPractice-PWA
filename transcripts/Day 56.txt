 Day 56. Grit. Swap and conquer. You know how sometimes you're stuck on a problem, and it just feels like you're spinning your wheels? Here's a fun twist to try out. Team up with a friend, and swap a tricky challenge you're each dealing with. It's like taking a break from your own puzzle and diving into theirs. You might be surprised at how seeing their challenge from a fresh angle gives you new insights. Kind of like a mental cross-training. Plus, helping each other out, it's a double win. You both get a shot of confidence, shake off that stuck feeling, and flex those problem-solving muscles in a whole new way. Give it a go, and watch how your grit grows. Together, you're stronger.
