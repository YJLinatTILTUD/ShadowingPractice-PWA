 Day 82. Conquering adversity. The miracle of moving. Feeling overwhelmed with your studies, work, and life's hustle? Instead of just thinking about it, why not try some physical activity? Of course, it's easy to think, I don't have time for exercise or anything else. But guess what? That type of thinking is exactly what's holding you back in life. In fact, the busier or more challenging life is, the more you need to move your body and get your heart pumping. Get up from your desk and stretch. Go for a short 20-minute walk. Or do something else to get your body active. I know it might sound overly simplistic, but there's actual science behind this advice. Physical activity has been shown to be able to totally transform your mood and give you a fresh new perspective on whatever's been worrying you. You'll find problems that initially seemed massive, shrink, or even disappear. By moving, you're giving yourself a little break to refresh. So remember, next time you're feeling stuck or snowed under, just get moving. A little activity can really work wonders.
