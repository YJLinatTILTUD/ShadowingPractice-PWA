 Day 34. Goals and Dreams. Don't just focus on, what's in it for me? Listen. When it comes to goals, it's not just about the end game, the thing you want to have, or the place you want to get to. No, it's way deeper than that. The true value is who you become while you're reaching for that goal. I mean, sure, scoring that college acceptance or getting that job is awesome, but the person you evolve into, that's the real prize. You learn, you struggle, you adapt, and all that shapes you into someone better. So don't just focus on the what will I get part. Shift that thinking and ask yourself, what will I become? Because trust me, the growth along the way, that's what you'll carry with you long after you've achieved that goal.
