 Day 36. Grit. Leave doubt behind you. Picture life as a long marathon, filled with the highs of achievement and the lows of uncertainties. Now, imagine your dreams as the finish line you're working to reach. The pace you run this marathon is not about speed, but endurance, and that enduring pace is fuelled by grit. Grit keeps your legs moving when doubt tries to slow you down. With every step taken with grit, doubt falls further behind, and your dreams come closer. It's about outrunning the whisper of doubt with a loud echo of grit every step of the way. So, put on your shoes, set a pace filled with determination, and run towards your dreams, leaving doubt behind.
