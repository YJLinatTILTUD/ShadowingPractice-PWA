 Day 94. Goals and dreams. The past doesn't have to equal the future. I've got some great news. Your past doesn't have to define your future. Not one bit. Yeah, you might have messed up before, but guess what? Most of the super successful people you hear about started with nothing. They were not born rich, they earned it. And most of them messed up lots before they hit it big. Don't be fooled into thinking they just got lucky. They worked really hard for years to achieve what they now have. So don't get stuck on past results. Like poor grades, going to a poorly ranked college, being in a low position at work, etc. Thinking that's all you can do. Your future potential is limitless. So aim high, aim for what you really want, not just what you think you can get based on your past. Trust me, you'll be glad you did.
