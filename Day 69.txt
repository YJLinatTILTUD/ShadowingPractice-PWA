 Day 69. Self-Care. Silence the inner critic. Hey, you know being too hard on yourself with self-criticism is like having a downer soundtrack in your mind? It doesn't really help. It just makes you more stressed. It's like trying to make something cool in pottery class while a voice keeps nagging that you're doing it wrong. Try this. When you start being tough on yourself, pause and think, would I say this to my best friend? Like, if you mess up and think, I'm a failure, stop and ask yourself if you'd ever say that to a friend. You'd probably say, mistakes happen. Let's fix it. This friend test changes how you talk to yourself, from criticism to support. It's like giving yourself a little pep talk. Remember, always be as kind to yourself as you would to a friend. You deserve it.
